# Recollect AI Journal - Your Personal AI-Powered Diary

Welcome to **Recollect AI Journal**, your smart and intuitive journaling app. Keep track of your thoughts, goals, and memories with the power of AI, all while enjoying a sleek and simple user experience.

## Why Choose Recollect AI Journal?

- **AI-Powered Insights**: Gain meaningful insights from your entries. Our AI helps you track moods, trends, and personal growth.
- **Simple & Clean Design**: The interface is designed to be distraction-free, allowing you to focus on what really matters: your journaling.
- **Comprehensive Search**: Find any past entry with ease using advanced search features powered by AI.
- **Cross-Platform Sync**: Access your journal on all your devices with seamless syncing.

## Features

- **Daily Journal Prompts**: Get thoughtful prompts to help you reflect and write daily.
- **Secure & Private**: Your privacy is important to us. All your entries are securely stored, and you can enable password protection for added security.
- **Mood Tracking**: Track your emotional journey over time with automatic mood detection.
- **Personalized Experience**: The more you journal, the more personalized the app becomes, offering suggestions based on your unique journey.
